Welcome to Treasure Guessing Game!

Guess a letter from A-Z in order to get the tressure!

Game Mechanics:

To play the game, you have to guess a letter from A to Z. The input must be a letter; Entering a non letter type would make a deduction to your score 😐 Once you click the 'Check' button and if your guess is incorrect, there will be a message telling you either 'Too high' or 'Too Low' from the secret letter. If you get 'Too High'(👆), it means your guessed letter's index is beyond the secret letter's index. Same logic applies if you get a message saying 'Too Low'(👇), but just the opposite. Enjoy playing! 🥳
